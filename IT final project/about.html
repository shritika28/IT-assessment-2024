About us

Gauchan Airlines is a fictional company imagined for the sake of this project. We provide imaginary airline services. Right now, we only provide domestic airline services with economy, business and first class seats. We value our imaginary customers and try to do what's best for them. We are soon planning to expand our services to helicopter and private jet services.
